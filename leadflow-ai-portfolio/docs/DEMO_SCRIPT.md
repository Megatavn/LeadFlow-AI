# LeadFlow AI v2.5 — Demo Script

Recommended length: 45–60 seconds.

## Flow

1. Open the APK.
2. Show the onboarding slides with illustrations.
3. Enter Dashboard and point out Daily Brief + Sales Intelligence.
4. Tap the priority lead.
5. Show Lead Detail bottom sheet animation, avatar, AI Summary, Deal Probability, Lead Health, Timeline, and Next Best Action.
6. Tap “Tạo tin nhắn”.
7. Copy a follow-up message and show the toast feedback.
8. Open Pipeline and show the sales stages.
9. Open CV tab and show the Case Study screen.

## Voiceover idea

```text
LeadFlow AI is an offline Sales Intelligence CRM prototype I built for portfolio review. It helps small businesses and real estate agents manage leads, score intent, estimate deal probability, plan follow-up actions, handle objections, and generate sales messages. The project is intentionally offline-first with no backend, login, or external API, focusing on mobile UX, product logic, local storage, and Android APK packaging.
```
